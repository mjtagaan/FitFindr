About FitFindr

Why I Built This

As a student, I've struggled to find the "perfect" gym that fits my budget, schedule, and training needs. Between classes, assignments, and student life, finding a gym that actually works for my circumstances felt like an impossible task.

So I decided to do something about it. FitFindr is my personal project—a place where I document and review every gym I've personally tried. Each review is honest, detailed, and written from a student's perspective, presented in a fun and "techy" way.

My goal? To help other people—especially students—find gyms that actually suit their needs and circumstances, without the endless scrolling and guesswork.

What You'll Find Here

Honest Reviews

Real experiences from someone who's actually been there, worked out there, and knows what it's like.

Student-Friendly Focus

Special attention to pricing, student discounts, flexible hours, and budget-conscious options.

Detailed Insights

From equipment quality to crowd levels, operating hours to amenities—everything you need to know.
